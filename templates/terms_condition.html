2. Description of Service

Personal Debt Manager is a web-based application designed to help users track and manage personal loans and debts. The service allows users to:

  • Record debtor information and contact details
  • Track debit and credit transactions with automated calculations
  • Generate comprehensive reports and analytics
  • Export data for personal records and backup
  • Manage debt recovery processes and payment tracking
Note: This application is a record-keeping tool only and does not constitute financial, legal, or investment advice.
